Aboute me

When I meet new people or old friends, they are always shocked. when I tell them I’m studying software. They all see a girl that crochets when she travels, whose notebook is more doodle than note and posts about her glass beads every week, wouldn’t she be studying something more creative? No, because to me, code is a medium, just like yarn and glass. With software I get to create anything! Just look at this website, I created this. If that isn’t creative I don’t know what is.

Aside from the creative aspect, I love the problem solving that coding brings. It keeps every project unique and interesting.
